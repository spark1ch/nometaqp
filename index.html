No Meta Questions, Please

The person who shared this link prefers direct communication without meta-questions. Please ask your complete question right away.

About This Preference

Meta-questions are questions about questions, like "Can I ask you something?" instead of just asking what you want to know. They create unnecessary steps in communication.

By sharing this link, this person is requesting that you skip the meta-questions and get straight to the point.

Don't Do This

Are you there?
Can I ask you a question?
Do you have a minute?
Hi, are you busy?

Do This Instead

Hi, do you have the project files for the client meeting?
Can you review this document by Friday?
What time works for you to discuss the budget?
I need help with the server configuration.
